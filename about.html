About Us

The Aggie Tech Shop

The Aggie Tech Shop was founded by Jamaal Moore and is located in the heart of the Illustrious North Carlina Agricultural & Technical State University. The rental office is located on the 2nd floor of Smith Hall and is available from 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM Monday through Thursday and 9:30 AM - 2:30 PM Friday through Sunday. The Aggie Tech Shop provides a variety of devices with a range of afforable prices. Students and administration can schedule the duration of their rental and select specific devices through our site. We understand that everyone may not have the necessary resources available at their leisure and we aim to provide a solution to their problem.
